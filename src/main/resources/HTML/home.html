Hot Chicken Takeover Review

Blaise Herritt

I don't come to Columbus often and was only here for the weekend but I'm so glad my friends chose this place to have brunch. I tried the chicken sandwich and the reason it's called "hot" chicken takeover is because you can choose your heat level. I tried the hot which is 3 out 4 on the heat scale. I don't usually get spicy as restaurants claim it's hot when it's usually not but holy cow! I took two bites and my mouth was on fire. Of course, I like to suffer a little bit when I ask for spicy so it was perfect. But beware if you're not a spicy fan. Even the cold is really flavorful but has a little kick.
